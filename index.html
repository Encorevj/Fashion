WHAT IS FASHION BABA


Fashion is about expressing yourself & not just looking good. Fashion is about what you believe in & what you stand for. Sustainable fashion is need of the hour, where you can style yourself, as well as support the enironment. fashion is about co-existence. The idea is to adopt a Sustainable lifestyle in everything and letting go of fast fashion, which is 2nd most pollution causing industry in the world.


    MISSION
Our mission is to provide a Sustainablefashion brand, to support designers by providing them an accesible platform to showcase and grow in the indusrty.


    VISION
Our vision is to be the go to brand for Sustainablefashion & cater to the needs of every consumers who wants to adopt a Sustainable lifestyle. We aim to build a strong community of designers, enthusiasts & influencers promoting and preaching Sustainable fashion.
